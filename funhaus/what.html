What we do

We help people reach their needs and their goals by creating custom made wheelchairs

We here at funhaüs2 help encourage disabled people to do the things that they love. This could be running, playing sports, flying kites, etc. As long as we can help them reach their goal, we will have succeeded. We use very high quality parts and custom fit them for every wheelchair that needs them. There is no goal too hard for us that we can help you accomplish.
