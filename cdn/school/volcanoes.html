Eyjafjallajökull


Try saying that five times fast- Albert Einstein

Eyjafjallajökull is a volcano in Iceland. Its most recent eruption was on March 20th, 2010. This happened after nearly 3000 earthquakes hit the area and made it erupt. It erupted constantly from March 20th to May 23rd, 2010.


Video of the Eruption




The eruption of Eyjafjallajökull caused many delays in airlines. Even Airlines that don't go to Iceland were delayed because of the giant ash cloud that was covering the upper portions of Europe and North America. Because of this, other flights were rectroactivley cancelled because of saftey reasons arising from the ash cloud. These delays and cencellations ended on April 20th, 2010. According to the Insitute of Earth Sciences at the University of Iceland and the Icelandic Meteorological Office declared the eruption over in October of the same year. This volcanic eruption affected tens of thousands of travellers, and an event of this size will probably not happen for a long time.
